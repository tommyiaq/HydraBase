Why Hydrabase feels different

Instead of guessing moisture with a low-cost soil sensor, Hydrabase reads the most reliable signal: how much water the plant system has actually lost.

💧

Real physical signal

Water has weight. As the plant dries out, Hydrabase can detect the change directly and react with more consistency.

🪴

Works under the pot

No probes to insert, corrode, or reposition. Just place the pot on the base and let the system observe over time.

🤖

Ready for automation

Perfect as the intelligence layer for alerts, auto-watering routines, or future robotic watering systems.

How it works

Simple on the outside, smart on the inside. Hydrabase turns weight data into useful care decisions.

1
Place the plant on Hydrabase

The saucer-shaped base becomes the measurement point for the entire pot and water system.

2
Learn the baseline

After watering, the system can understand the high-water state and track the natural decline over time.

3
Trigger the next action

When the weight reaches a dynamic dry threshold, Hydrabase can notify the user or activate a watering workflow.
