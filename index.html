Finally, plants that can complain

You'd remember to water if your plant could text you. Hydrabase is the next best thing — it reads the most reliable signal there is: weight.

No lies from the soil

Soil sensors can read "dry" even when the bottom half is soaking wet. Weight doesn't lie — if the water is gone, you'll know.

Nothing to break (or lose)

No probe to insert, corrode, accidentally snap off, or throw in the trash during a repot. Just a base. Under the pot. Done.

It can do the watering too

Hook it up to an auto-watering system and go on vacation without asking your neighbour to "just check on the plants."

Dead simple to set up

If you can put a pot on a table, you can use Hydrabase. Seriously, that's basically all of step one.

1
Plop the plant on Hydrabase

The saucer-shaped base becomes the measurement point for the entire pot and water system. Setup complete.

2
Water it once (yes, just once)

Hydrabase locks in the "happy" weight and starts tracking the natural decline from there.

3
Get notified when it's actually thirsty

No more "should I water it? probably fine. maybe not." Just a clear signal — water now, or wait.
